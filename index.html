About Me

I'm a Neurologist specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases or impaired function of the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, muscles, autonomic nervous system, and blood vessels that relate to these structures. I am passionate about advancing our understanding through research, education, and innovation. My work focuses on interdisciplinary approaches to solving complex problems and contributing to the scientific community.

Clinical-Research Philosophy

My approach to medical care is based on evidence-based scholarship translating advanced theoretical understanding of pathological conditions into effective clinical practice and effective healthcare delivery. I believe in the power of collaborative science and open research practices to advance knowledge and benefit society.

Fundraising

Dr. Dinov is cycling toward a world free of MS

Dr. Dinov signed up for Bike MS with the UTSW TEAM to ride and raises funds for the National Multiple Sclerosis Society. This organization has paved the way for every effective MS treatment available today — the Society is the best investment in reaching a cure for multiple sclerosis. Personally for me I think this is a great way to both continue supporting my own health while supporting an organization I am very passionate about!

Multiple sclerosis (MS) is a devastating, unpredictable disease and no one should face it alone. Every mile we pedal and every dollar we raise brings us closer to a world free of MS. I’m going the distance with my training and commitment to end MS, and I hope I can count on your support as I prepare for this incredible experience. I’ve set an ambitious goal because I know that with support from people like you, I can get there.

Join me on this journey and make a difference today. Donate and help me reach my goal
